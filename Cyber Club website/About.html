About Cyber Club

The Cyber Club is a community dedicated to promoting knowledge and skills in cybersecurity and information technology. Our mission is to empower members with the tools and resources needed to navigate the digital world safely and effectively.

Objective

Our primary objective is to educate and raise awareness about cybersecurity issues, provide hands-on experience through workshops and projects, and create a supportive environment for members to learn and grow.

Activities

  • Workshops on cybersecurity fundamentals
  • Guest lectures from industry professionals
  • Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions
  • Networking events and hackathons
  • Community outreach and awareness programs
